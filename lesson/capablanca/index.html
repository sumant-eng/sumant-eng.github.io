About this course

Jose Raul Capablanca

In this course “How to play like Capablanca” you will learn to master simple positions.

A chess prodigy from Cuba, Capablanca is widely known for his amazing endgame skills and his mastery of simple positions. He preferred to only spend time thinking about what he would do in critical positions instead of calculating long variations.

By learning his style of play, you will be better able to identify critical positions in your own games and learn how to strategize plans which you would then follow through until the end without too many variations.
