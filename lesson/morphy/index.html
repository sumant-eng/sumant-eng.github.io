Paul Morphy

You must attend the course “How to play like Paul Morphy” if you are a beginner to the game of chess or are just a big fan of his style of play! Learning his style will educate you to be a player with the healthiest and strongest fundamentals in chess.

I have not yet come across a chess protegee that has not heard of the genius of Paul Morphy. I was 11 years old when I first came across Paul Morphy’s name. It happened by chance as I was browsing the internet for the most dominant chess player with the highest win-rate.

I remember studying his games and repeatedly come to the conclusion that he must be the strongest player of all time! I did not particularly enjoy Fischer’s or Karpov’s games when I was a child as they were too complicated for me to understand and draw inspiration from.
