Be Good at Math, Simple Techniques!

Many students feel they are not good at mathematics. It’s true, but only if you think it is!

As a Rotocraft Engineer and a lecturer for engineering students, being good at mathematics is not an option. I have to solve engineering problems almost every day.

When I was in primary school, I was fortunate not trapped in the myth that math is hard. I learned mathematics as same as the other subjects.

Here are some tips I like to share with you on how to study and become good at mathematics.

Study in a quiet environment

Go to a place like a library or your room with less distraction. Don’t listen to any music while doing mathematics. Switch off your handphone and TV.

You’ve to study in an environment that allows you to get the concentrate.

Study everyday

You don’t have to spend hours every day to study mathematics. You only need to discipline yourself to study mathematics at least an hour a day but consistently.

My advice is to study every day at the same time and in the same place. You’ll get focus much faster in each session.

Practice answering questions or problems

Open the questions and answer them. By far, this is the best method for me. I believe it’s the best for you either.

If you get stuck, then only you check your notes or get some help from your teacher or friends.

Now try the tips. I bet you will get better at mathematics in no time. Good luck!
